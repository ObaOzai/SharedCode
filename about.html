The Astro Pema AI Mythopoethic Project:


A Five-Decade Journey


The Astro Pema project represents the evolution of a vision that began in the mid-1970s when I first encountered astrology and completed Max Heindel's Rosicrucian correspondence course. In those early days, astrological calculations required paper ephemerides, slide rules, and the revolutionary Texas Instruments calculators that had just been released.


During my college years in the mid-1980s, I attempted to digitize this work using Pascal programming skills. Even with the emergence of personal computers, I was still constrained by the need for physical ephemeris tables and manual calculations.


Today, Astro Pema has transformed into a sophisticated computational system. We utilize the Swiss Ephemeris as resident server files, performing all astronomical calculations through our proprietary algorithms. Most significantly, we've integrated Small Language Models (SLMs) to generate uniquely original narratives for each individual birth configuration, moving beyond traditional cookbook interpretations.


While the work continues to evolve, I'm confident this iteration will achieve what previous attempts could not: a complete astrological system founded on original mythopoetic narratives that honor both the mathematical precision of celestial mechanics and the archetypal richness of astrological symbolism.


This represents not just a technological advancement, but the fulfillment of a vision nearly fifty years in the making.


Astro Pema runs on a high-performance workstation with AI acceleration capabilities:


  • CPU: Intel Core i9-14900F (24 cores, 32 threads, up to 5.8 GHz)
  • Memory: 32GB DDR5-6000 (expandable to 64GB)
  • GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 5070 (12GB VRAM) with CUDA 12.9 support
  • Cache: 36MB L3, 32MB L2, optimized for AI workloads
  • Architecture: x86_64 with VT-x virtualization
  • AI Stack: Python environments with GPU acceleration for chart calculations
  • Services: Web server, mail server, and LLM inference engine
